Important note

The official BPRS is a clinician-rated psychiatric symptom scale. In this public-facing prototype, the same 18 symptom domains are used as a guided pre-assessment inside an AI-assisted workflow to support conversation and triage discussion. It does not replace clinical judgment, diagnosis, or emergency assessment.

  • Use this separately from the staff portal to show a simple access point for the audience.
  • If immediate safety risk is present, use local emergency services or the nearest emergency department.
  • The AI workflow in this prototype uses structured triage logic and scoring summaries for presentation.
  • If the score is above 40, the saved workflow can warn the psychiatrist team through Telegram.
